JEXTOL PAINT

Jextol Paint is manufactured by Chi'adoram Concept Enterprises. Jextol Paint is an outstanding paint which can match any paint manufactured in Nigeria in terms of quality even at a reduced price. This makes our product attractive to those that value quality even in a harsh economic climate as Nigeria.
For anyone interested in quality paint product that is durable lasting up to ten years Jextol paint is the name to call. Our paint is relatively cheaper despite it high quality. Jextol paint adds beauty and protection against harsh weather conditional at a cheaper rate.

JEXTOL RANGE OF PRODUCT INCLUDES:

Emulsion
Satin
Weather Guard
Gloss
Matt
And various kinds of textured paint

JEXTOL PREMIUM EMULSION

Jextol Premium Emulsion has a matt finish surface when it is applied on the wall. It is fast drying with over 10sqm per litre in terms of spread. It is parked with sufficient fungicides to fight off mould, algae and other bacteria. It has the capacity to last over ten years.

JEXTOL WEATHER GUARD

Jextol Weather Guard is comparable to Dulux Weather Shield. It has a sheen effect and drives very fast. It is produced with high grade pigment and strong biocide to protect the wall from fungi and other bacteria.

JEXTOL GOLD EMULSION

Jextol Gold Emulsion is a high quality emulsion produced for or the extractr. It has a good resistance to algae and fungal attacks.

JEXTOL STANDARD EMULSION

Jextol Standard Emulsion has a very good spread of over 10sqm per litre.

JEXTOL GLOSS

This is a high built and viscosity paint. It is weather resistant and has a long lasting protection for wood, metal and plastered.

JEXTOL SATIN

Jextol Satin has a very high sheen effect on any surface it is applied on. It is stain resistant; you can easily wash off stains from any surface it is applied. It spreads well and dries in less than an hour.
